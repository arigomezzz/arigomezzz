About Me

I am an undergraduate Information Science major and a Media and Journalism minor at UNC Chapel Hill. I was born in Bolivia but moved to the US when I was young. I grew up in Raleigh, North Carolina and am a proud Tar Heel! I hope to pursue a career in UX/UI design or go into tech consulting. In my free time, I enjoy exploring new design trends in art and experiment with digital art. I'm passionate about creating user-centered experiences that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. On campus, I'm involved with KTP and am a part of a first-generation college students club. My multicultural background has given me a unique perspective that I bring to all my projects, allowing me to approach problems with diverse insights. I'm currently seeking internship opportunities where I can apply my skills and continue growing as a designer and problem solver.
